The move to screen mediation, no doubt, changed our identities, sensabilities, and relationship with the world. A host of shifts followed: online exchanges, remix culture and aetsthetics, prosumer creativity, and ephemerality.

And this new stance has emerged from a framework of words and print, at least where writing and scholarship are at play. This collection of videos captures some of the back and forth as methods of scholarly production merge with the screen.
