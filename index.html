Video Scholarship and Screen Composing

This collection brings together multiple examples of scholarship in the form of digital videos. For decades, from the early days of hypertext to the heyday of the web to emerging engagements with data, scholars and teachers have been studying aspects of multimedia writing. Much less frequently, however, do we find examples of research and scholarship conducted through multimedia. The pieces collected here seek to fill that vacuum.
